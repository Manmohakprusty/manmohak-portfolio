Process
How I approach projects

A consistent, security-aware and UX-driven way of working from idea to launch.

1. Understand & Define
Clarify the problem, audience, and constraints. Map user journeys, security risks, and success metrics before touching UI or code.
2. Design & Prototype
Draft low-fi flows and layouts, then refine them into clear, guided experiences — especially around forms, error states, and edge cases.
3. Build & Iterate
Implement with clean, reusable components, test across devices, and improve based on feedback, performance, and security checks.
